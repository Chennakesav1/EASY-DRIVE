Help Center

What if my bike won't start?
Check if the side-stand is down; most EVs have a safety cut-off. Ensure the kill switch is 'On' and the battery is above 10%.
How do I charge at home?
Use the provided 5A charger. Connect the charger to the bike first, then plug into a standard 3-pin wall socket.
What is the security deposit?
The ₹1000 deposit is fully refundable. It is processed within 7 working days once the vehicle is returned in good condition.
