Our Journey of Growth

From a small workshop to a global leader in industrial fasteners.

2010

PreciFast Founded

Started in a small 500 sq. ft. workshop with just two manual lathe machines and a vision to provide quality fasteners to local mechanics.

2015

First CNC Machine Installed

Upgraded our manufacturing capabilities by introducing high-precision CNC machines, increasing our production output by 300%.

2020

ISO Certification & Expansion

Achieved ISO 9001 quality certification and expanded our factory footprint to 10,000 sq. ft. to handle bulk industrial orders.

2026

Global Export Network

Launched our global supply chain, exporting high-tensile bolts to the automotive and aerospace industries across 15 countries.

How We Build Precision

Our state-of-the-art 4-step manufacturing process ensures every bolt meets rigorous global standards.

⚙️

1. Material Selection

Sourcing premium grade steel alloys for maximum tensile strength and durability.

🔥

2. Cold Forging

Shaping the bolt head with extreme pressure to guarantee structural integrity.

🔩

3. Thread Rolling

Precision threading ensures a perfect, vibration-resistant fit for heavy machinery.

🔬

4. Quality Testing

Rigorous stress, torque, and sheer testing to meet and exceed ISO certifications.

Industries We Serve

Engineering tailored fastening solutions for the world's most demanding sectors.

Automotive

High-tensile Grade 10.9 and 12.9 bolts designed for engine assembly, chassis, and extreme vibration resistance.

Aerospace

Lightweight, titanium-alloy fasteners built to withstand extreme pressure and rapid temperature variations.

Heavy Construction

Heavy-duty structural bolts (A325 & A490) engineered for skyscrapers, bridges, and critical infrastructure.

Marine & Offshore

Corrosion-resistant stainless steel and galvanized fasteners perfect for oil rigs and shipbuilding.
